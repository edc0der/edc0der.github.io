Word Counter

A focused, single-screen word counter for iOS and Android. Open the app, start typing. A live stats island shows your word and character counts in real time. Tap the chart icon to expand it into a full stats card — words, characters, sentences, paragraphs, read time, and chars without spaces. No accounts. No sync. Always starts fresh.

KMP SwiftUI Jetpack Compose iOS 17+ Android 13+

Features

Everything you need, nothing you don't.

Live Stats

Word count, character count, sentences, paragraphs, and read time — all calculated instantly as you type.

Animated Stats Island

A floating pill at the bottom of the screen expands with a spring animation into a detailed stats card — and collapses back just as smoothly.

Rich Text

Apply bold, italic, and header formatting via a clean toolbar. The stats always reflect the underlying plain text.

Share Stats

Send your stats snapshot to any app with the native share sheet — copy to clipboard, send a message, or save to Notes.

Stateless

No persistence, no history, no account. Kill the app and reopen: blank slate. Focused on the task at hand.

Truly Cross-Platform

SwiftUI on iOS. Jetpack Compose on Android. Shared business logic via Kotlin Multiplatform — native UI on each platform, no compromises.
