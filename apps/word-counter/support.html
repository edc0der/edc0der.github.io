Support for Word Counter — Text Stats for iOS & Android

Need help with Word Counter?
Word Counter is a simple text statistics app for checking words, characters, sentences, paragraphs, and estimated reading time. This page covers common questions, purchase help, privacy, and how to contact support.

Contact Support

For bugs, purchase issues, feedback, or feature requests, email us directly. Please include the device model, operating system version, app version, and a short description of the issue.

Email
edc0der.apps@gmail.com

Before You Contact Us

For counting issues: copy the text somewhere safe, clear the app, paste it again, and check whether the result changes.

For ads or purchase issues: open Settings in the app and use Restore Purchases.

For app issues: close and reopen the app, then check whether an update is available in the App Store or Google Play.

Frequently Asked Questions

What can I use Word Counter for?

Use Word Counter to quickly check text length for essays, posts, notes, messages, articles, product descriptions, and other writing. The app shows instant statistics without requiring an account.

Which statistics does the app show?

Word Counter can show words, characters, sentences, paragraphs, and estimated reading time. The exact statistics available may vary by app version and platform.

Does the app save, upload, or share my text?

No. Word Counter is designed to process your text on your device. Your text is not uploaded to our servers. For more detail, read the Privacy Policy.

How do I remove ads?

Open Settings in Word Counter and choose the ad removal option. If available in your version, this is a one-time purchase that removes ads permanently for the account used to make the purchase.

I paid to remove ads but still see them. What should I do?

Open Settings and tap Restore Purchases. Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID or Google account used for the original purchase. If the issue continues, email support and include the platform, device model, and app version.

Why does the count differ from another app or website?

Different tools may handle punctuation, emojis, symbols, line breaks, contractions, URLs, or non-Latin scripts differently. If you find a case that looks wrong, send us a short sample text so we can review it.

Can I request a feature?

Yes. Send feedback by email and describe what you are trying to accomplish. We review suggestions for future updates.
